Anubhav's Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tej Patta
  • Green Chilli
  • Mustard Oil
  • Haldi Powder
  • Lal Mirchi Powder
  • Kashmiri Mirch Powder
  • Jeera Powder
  • Kali Mirch Powder
  • Garam Masala
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Cut Onions, lambe-lambe and keep them aside
  2. Grind garlic and green chilli in a mixer
  3. Ab cooker chadhao and sabse pehle tel daalo
  4. Tel garam hone k baad, usme 2-3 tej patta daalo aur thoda sa pyaaz daal k fry karo
  5. Pyaaz brown hone k baad usme lahsun aur mirchi ka paste mila do aur thoda bhuno
  6. chiken daalo and ab phir se 3-4 minute fry karo taki chiken k pieces lahsun k sath fry ho jaye
  7. Baki k sare pyaaz daal do ab
  8. Bari Baari se saare masaale aur namak daal do. Garam masala abhi nahi daalna
  9. 1 min fry kar k paani daal do aur cooker band kar do
  10. 5 seeti k baad cooker kholo, garam masala daalo aur phir dhakkan khol k hi pakaao
  11. Ras jab gadha ho jaye toh gas band kar k cooker dhak do thodi der 5-10 minute k liye
  12. phir nikal k khao :)
